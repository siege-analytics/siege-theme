About -- A where-first firm

We think like geographers.

Siege Analytics is a strategic consultancy whose expertise usually shows up as technology. We started in the highest-stakes corner of data work -- political campaigns and international humanitarian projects -- and built a method around a single conviction: where people live is the biggest clue to who they are and how they'll act.

Ten-plus years and five continents later, that method holds up just as well for a company choosing store locations or a nonprofit mapping its members as it does for a campaign finding its voters. We are a 100% minority-owned firm based in Austin, Texas -- and we treat technology as the practice of enablement: making things visible and doable that weren't before.
