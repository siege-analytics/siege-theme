Signature

Political Financial Analysis

Powers elect.info

Money leaves a trail, and the trail is public -- if you can wrangle it. We work across federal, state and local campaign-finance records at once, resolving the same donors, committees and vendors into single entities even when every jurisdiction files them differently. Then we match that resolved money-graph to your BI lists or voterfiles. It is the engine behind elect.info, our own project.

Federal, state & local filings
Cross-jurisdiction entity resolution
Matching to voterfiles & BI lists
Donor & PAC network analysis
