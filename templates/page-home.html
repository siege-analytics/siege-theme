Our thesis

The choropleth is the easy part.

The hard part is the spatial database underneath. Matching voter files to precincts, reconciling Census geographies across vintages, building crosswalks when the boundaries shift mid-cycle.

We do the infrastructure work that makes the map possible. Our clients get answers that hold up because the data underneath was built to withstand scrutiny.
